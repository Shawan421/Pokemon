What is Pokémon evolution?

In Pokémon, evolution is the process where a Pokémon transforms into a stronger, more advanced form within its evolutionary line. This transformation typically involves a change in physical appearance, increased stats, and sometimes a new or improved movepool. Evolution is a core mechanic in the games, offering players a way to make their Pokémon more powerful and complete their Pokédex entries.

Special Evolutions

There are special evolutions that are different from normal evolutions.

For example: branch-evolutions,split-mega evolutions,mega evolutions,stellar evolutions,Eeveelution,dynamax and gigantamax.

Branch-evolutions

A split evolution is a Pokémon evolution that can turn the previous creature into one of a few different forms. These types of evolutions can occur if the player meets a set of specific criteria, or they can choose by using a certain item.

Mega evolutions

In the Pokémon games, Mega Evolution is a temporary, powerful evolution form that certain Pokémon can achieve, typically involving a visual transformation and increased stats. It's triggered by a Pokémon holding its specific Mega Stone while being held in battle by the Trainer, and can last for a single battle or until the Mega-Evolved Pokémon faints.

Mega stone

In the Pokémon games, a Mega Stone is a specific item that, when held by a compatible Pokémon and used with a Key Stone, triggers Mega Evolution, a temporary transformation that boosts the Pokémon's stats and changes its appearance. These stones are tied to specific Pokémon, allowing them to evolve into a Mega form.

Stellar evolutions

Stellar evolution grants the power boost to all types of moves, but only once per type, unless in Tera Raid Battles where all moves are boosted without limit. It also changes the effects of Tera Blast, making it more powerful and super effective against other Terastallized Pokémon.

Stellar tera shards

Stellar Tera Shards in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet are a type-specific item used to change a Pokémon's Tera Type to the Stellar type. Once you gather enough (50 of a specific type), you can exchange them at the Treasure Eatery in Medali, allowing you to change a Pokémon's Tera Type to Stellar.

Eeveelution

'Eeveelution' refers to any of the eight different Pokémon that evolve from Eevee, a unique Pokémon known for its diverse evolutionary potential. The term is a portmanteau of "Eevee" and "evolution," and it's used by fans to collectively refer to Eevee and its evolved forms.

Dynamax

n Pokémon, "Dynamax" refers to a temporary phenomenon where a Pokémon grows to a gigantic size and increases in power, primarily seen in the Galar region. It's not a true evolution like Mega Evolution, but a form that lasts for a short period in battle. During Dynamaxing, a Pokémon's moves transform into powerful Max Moves, and they also gain increased stats.

Gigantamax

Gigantamax is a special form of Dynamaxing in Pokémon Sword and Shield that changes both the size and appearance of certain Pokémon. It's not just a size increase; it also alters the Pokémon's visual design and introduces unique, powerful moves called G-Max Moves.

Evolution Stone

Evolution Stones are stones with the power of a certain element that can instantly evolve certain Pokémon they are used on

There are ten types of Evolutionary Stones in total

What are Legendary Pokémon?

Legendary Pokémon are very rare Pokémon in the Pokémon world. Characters in the series often claim that very few people have seen them. They are hard to catch, and are stronger than most other Pokémon. They have powers that other Pokémon do not have.

What are the Ultra Beasts?

Ultra Beasts, introduced in Pokémon Sun and Moon, are a unique group of Pokémon that originate from a dimension called Ultra Space. They are often described as alien and powerful, and they can occasionally travel to the Pokémon world through Ultra Wormholes. They are not from our world and are considered Extradimensional Pokémon.
